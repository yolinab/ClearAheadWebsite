How it works

Simple by design.
Personal by nature.

01
Connect your calendar
Clear Ahead reads your schedule structure — meeting density, fragmentation, deep work windows. Never the content. Never the titles. Only the shape of your day.
02
The model learns you
A lightweight background tracker monitors your working patterns — session length, context switching, focus depth. From day one it applies cognitive science to your actual schedule. Over weeks it learns what drains you specifically.
03
Your morning briefing
Each morning, one clear picture of your cognitive capacity and what to do with it. On good days, where to place your hardest work. On difficult days, what to protect. Always yours. Always specific.

What you get

Not a tracker.
An intelligence layer.

Daily cognitive briefing
Every morning before your day starts — capacity outlook, what your schedule costs, and one specific adjustment. Useful whether the news is good or not.
Pattern recognition
Your model surfaces what you cannot see yourself — the meeting sequence that destroys your Thursday, the overload that has been quietly building for nine days.
Burnout early warning
With enough longitudinal data, the system detects trajectories, not just states. Weeks of warning, not days of damage control.
Personal baseline model
Not compared to others. Measured against yourself. The model gets more accurate the longer it runs — building a portrait of your cognitive life no other tool can offer.
